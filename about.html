About Me

My name is Pedro Afonso, and I come from the city of Valongo, in the Metropolitan Area of Porto.

After a Bachelor's in Physics, I am currently in my first semester of the Copernicus Master in Digital Earth (CDE), where I’m filling the gaps between the World of Physics and the Geospatial & Earth Observation Domain. Outside of academia, I enjoy working on personal projects that blend data visualization, geography, and creative problem-solving.

When I’m not buried in code or maps, you can find me playing football, editing Wikipedia, or exploring the world through Geocaching.
