Network Analysis of Salzburg

The Network Analysis Tools of ArcGIS Online and ArcGIS Pro are incredibly versatile tools that make some complicated mathematical problems easily solvable with just a few clicks.

On this report, I explore several of them in the context of Tourist Attractions in the Austrian City of Salzburg.

Catchment Basin Analysis of the Thumersbach River

Continuing the ongoing exploration of ArcGIS Pro Tools, the focus this time was on Digital Elevation Models (DEMs) and their relation with river catchment basins. The study area was the basin of the Thumersbach River, and I explored the elevation and its derivatives, with special focus on the slope. In the end, I compared the results for lower-resolution DEMs.
